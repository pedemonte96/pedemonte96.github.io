Personal Profile

Hello! I am Martí Pedemonte Bernat. I consider myself an ambitious and hardworking individual, very curious and always eager to learn. It is the scientist in me that makes me want to find answers to unsolved questions, applying rigorous thinking and learning new abilities in the journey.
